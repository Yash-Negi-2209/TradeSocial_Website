About Us

Trade Social: Empowering Local Businesses in Aurangabad, Bihar


Trade Social was born out of a heartfelt desire to uplift the local business community in Aurangabad, Bihar. The story begins with Mr. Agrawal, a native of Aurangabad, who grew up witnessing the vibrant but often unadvertised local businesses in his hometown. After spending several years in the US, where he gained invaluable experience and insights in the world of business and technology, Mr. Agrawal decided it was time to give back to his roots. Upon his return to Aurangabad, Mr. Agrawal noticed a significant gap in the local market: a lack of effective advertising platforms for small businesses and services. He understood that these businesses, though rich in potential, were struggling to reach a wider audience and compete with larger entities. This realization sparked the inception of Trade Social.Trade Social was created with a simple yet powerful vision: to provide a dedicated platform where local residents could easily post sales, promote their goods, and connect with potential customers. It aims to democratize advertising, making it accessible and affordable for everyone in the community. Through Trade Social, local businesses in Aurangabad can now showcase their products, share special offers, and attract more customers than ever before. When its launched, Trade Social is aim to become a cornerstone for the local economy, fostering growth and enabling small businesses to thrive. By bridging the gap between sellers and buyers, the platform not only boosts the visibility of local enterprises but also strengthens the sense of community in Aurangabad. Mr. Agrawal's vision for Trade Social is rooted in his deep love for his hometown and his commitment to its prosperity. With Trade Social, he hopes to create a sustainable ecosystem where local businesses can flourish, contributing to the overall economic and social development of Aurangabad, Bihar.

Services

Trade Social provides a platform for merchants to post their products and promote their sales with customer interaction options.
